elimination of le with plus
Lemma Nle_plus : ∀ n k, k ≤ n → ∃ p, n = k + p.
intros n k.
intro.
induction H.
∃ 0. auto with arith.
destruct IHle.
rewrite H0.
∃ (S x).
auto with arith.
Qed.
