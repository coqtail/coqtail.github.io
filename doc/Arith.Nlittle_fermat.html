Fermat's little theorem over nat

Theorem Nlittle_fermat :
  ∀ a p, Nprime p → (p | a ^ p - a).

Alternative formulation of Fermat's little theorem

Theorem Nlittle_fermat_alt :
  ∀ a p, Nprime p → Nrel_prime a p →
  (p | a ^ (pred p) - 1).
